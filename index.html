Semester Diary

Oh my god! I have so many works to hand in!

Semester Diary allows students to manage assignments by each deadline. The user can keep track on the progress of each assignment by organizing everyday schedule..

  • Get Started

Tab 1. List

As soon as you log in, take a look at the list of assignments you have. They are in the order considering the deadline, workload, and how much it is completed. When adding a new assignment, you can add details about it. If you are working with other people, you may add their account and share the progess. Try to break down the assignment as detailed as possible, and do it one by one. It will suggest yourself much clear direction until you complete the assignment in time.

  • Go to top

Tab 2. Today

Semester Diary also suggests you what to do today. 'Write a report' is too ambiguous, making you feel a lot of pressure. Instead, now try to master subtask of writing a report step by step. Enjoy the excitement from the real time on-going progress you are making. This will keep your assignments and events organized until the final deadline, so that you don't face cram. Not sure where and what to start? Semester Diary will also give you some suggestions according to their importance.

  • Go to top

Tab 3. Calendar

Calendar with assignment deadlines and meeting dates gives you quick idea of the whole plan of the month. For example, if a lot of assignment deadlines are concentrated in one day, you will easily notice that it will be better to start early and keep up working on it with Today Tab. This will also help you a lot with arranging a meeting with others.
